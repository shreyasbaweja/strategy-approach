  • Forgetting to cancel vs actively choosing to
  • Pre-renewal reminders absent

Health Commerce — Domain Expertise

What differentiates a PM with subscription health experience from a generic growth background. The trust dimension changes everything.

🏥

Trust Dimension — Health vs Transactional Commerce

In fashion subscription, a customer can return a box if it's wrong. In health subscription, they trust you with their body, their confidence, and their outcomes. That changes how you think about every conversion decision.

You can't just optimise for click-through — you must optimise for clarity, reassurance, and genuine confidence at every touchpoint.

🔄

Break-In Phase = The Post-Acquisition Loyalty Problem

The break-in phase in prescription health — where initial adjustment is misread as product failure — is the direct equivalent of the 20% cancellation problem in subscription commerce: customer expectations misaligned with reality at the moment of first delivery.

The fix: Informative step pre-commitment + personalised break-in narrative post-onboarding.

📋

Medical Onboarding = High-Stakes Style Survey

Medical onboarding funnels are the primary conversion lever in prescription health — exactly the same mechanics as style-matching questionnaire flows, but with higher stakes and a clinical review layer.

  • Question phrasing affects completion rate
  • Adaptive branching improves outcome accuracy
  • Trust signals at anxiety points create measurable lift
🌍

Platform Vision — Cross-Vertical PM Thinking

Leading health commerce platforms are expanding from single verticals to full-spectrum health: dermatology → weight management → hormonal health → hair restoration. This is a platform-level CRO opportunity — not just a product.

The critical question: "How does the team think about shared experimentation infrastructure vs vertical-specific product decisions? That shapes how roadmap priorities compound — or stay siloed."

What "Best in Class CVR" Scenario Reveals

📈 Activation Quality First

Best-in-class acquisition CVR means nothing if 40% cancel during the break-in phase. Move immediately to month-1 activation — the gap between "signed up" and "positive first 30-day outcome."

💰 Plan Upgrade Surface

If acquisition is optimised, the frontier is AOV trajectory. Are customers on the entry plan being given a reason and moment to upgrade post-activation? The clinician relationship is an enormous trust asset for upsell.

🔍 Traffic Mix Quality

Even best-in-class overall CVR can hide a poorly-converting segment averaged with a strong one. Segment by acquisition channel before declaring success. Blended numbers hide broken channels.

"Most growth practitioners optimise for conversion rate. I think optimising for conversion rate is often the wrong goal. The metric I actually care about is quality-adjusted conversion — how many people we converted were right for the product. You should never want to convert a customer who isn't a good clinical fit, even if the funnel mechanics could push them through." — The differentiating principle on growth in high-trust subscription commerce.
